Dog

Dogs are descendants of wolves and have been human companions for thousands of years.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Lifespan: 10-13 years
  • Habitat: Domesticated environments
  • Breed Types: Various breeds with unique characteristics

Dogs are known for their loyalty and diverse roles as companions, working animals, and service animals. They have been domesticated from wolves and have played significant roles in human history. From tiny Chihuahuas to giant Great Danes, there is a wide variety of dog breeds, each with its own characteristics and traits.
